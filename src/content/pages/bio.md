---
title: "Bio"
---

Laura D’Angeli (1995) graduated in Fine Arts at the University of Bologna in 2017, and subsequently in Film and Media Studies at the University of Copenhagen in 2020, where she completed her thesis with the short documentary film Diciotto which premiered at the Nordisk Panorama Film Festival in Malmö.

In 2021, she worked as a programming assistant at the Cineteca di Bologna and won a research grant at the Biennale Cinema College ASAC in Venice. She writes for the film magazines Skuelyst and Filmidee.

Also in 2021, she enrolled in the documentary directing program at the Centro Sperimentale di Cinematografia in Palermo. There she co-directed the short documentary Real Guadagna.

She graduated from the Centro Sperimentale with the short documentary Io quando esco da qua in 2025.

She currently works as an expert trainer leading cinema workshops in public schools and in different film production sets.
